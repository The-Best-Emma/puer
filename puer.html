Brewing Guide

01
💧
Rinse the Cake
Break off 5–8g. Rinse with boiling water for 5 seconds to awaken the leaves and wash away any dust.
02
🌡️
Heat the Water
Use water at 95–100°C (203–212°F). Puerh is robust and requires near-boiling water to open fully.
03
⏳
Steep Briefly
First steep: 20–30 seconds. Add 10 seconds each subsequent steep. Aged cakes can be steeped 10–15 times.
04
🫖
Pour & Enjoy
Decant fully to stop steeping. Appreciate the color, aroma, and the lingering huigan — sweet finish.
